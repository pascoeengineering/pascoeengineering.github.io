Before You Reach Out

A few common questions.

Do I need to know which engineering service I require?

No. Describe your project, the property, and any requirements you have received. We can help determine what engineering support may be appropriate.

Can you review existing drawings or municipal comments?

Yes. Existing drawings, reports, surveys, and municipal comments can provide useful context when identifying practical next steps.

Do you work with both homeowners and development teams?

Yes. Pascoe Engineering supports homeowners, property owners, developers, builders, architects, planners, surveyors, and other project professionals.
