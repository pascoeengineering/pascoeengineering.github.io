About Pascoe Engineering

Practical civil engineering consulting supported by municipal development engineering experience.

Small Scale Projects Focused.

Pascoe Engineering was founded to provide homeowners and small developers with practical, reliable civil engineering consulting services. We understand that every project is unique, and we are committed to guiding our clients through the development and approval process with clear communication, responsive service, and practical engineering solutions. to make professional engineering services more accessible to homeowners and small developers. Whether it is a residential addition, pool grading plan, or a larger development project, we work closely with our clients to provide practical solutions and dependable guidance from concept through approval.
