About Pascoe Engineering

Every project deserves to matter.

Pascoe Engineering exists because homeowners and small developers deserve a civil engineering company that treats their projects with the same level of care, professionalism, and attention regardless of project size.

Why We Exist

Helping people move projects forward with confidence.

Engineering should make projects easier—not more complicated.

Engineering plays an important role in shaping our communities, but for many homeowners and small developers, finding the right engineering support can be surprisingly difficult.

Pascoe Engineering was founded to provide practical civil engineering services that are approachable, responsive, and focused on helping clients move confidently through the municipal approval process.

Whether the project involves a residential improvement, custom home, or small development, our goal remains the same: provide thoughtful engineering, clear communication, and dependable support from beginning to end.

Our Approach

Clear thinking, practical solutions, and dependable support.

Our approach is shaped by the belief that engineering should help clients understand their options, make informed decisions, and move their projects forward with confidence.

Practical

Solutions should work on paper and in the field.

We focus on engineering solutions that are technically sound, realistic to construct, and appropriate for the project.

Responsive

Communication should keep projects moving.

Clear updates and timely responses help clients understand where their project stands and what comes next.

Thoughtful

Every recommendation should be considered carefully.

We consider the property, project goals, approval requirements, and long-term success of the project before recommending a solution.

Dependable

Clients should know they can rely on us.

Our work is grounded in honest advice, quality engineering, and consistent support throughout the assignment.

Clear

Engineering should be easier to understand.

We explain technical information and municipal requirements in straightforward language so clients can make informed decisions.

Who We Work With

Collaborative engineering support for residential projects.

Pascoe Engineering works with property owners, small developers, and project teams that value clear communication, practical design, and coordinated decision-making.

Homeowners

Support for residential property improvements.

Helping homeowners navigate grading, drainage, pools, additions, driveways, permits, and other property-related projects.

Small Developers

Civil engineering for small residential developments.

Supporting development projects with grading, servicing, stormwater management, reports, and municipal coordination.

Industry Professionals

Responsive support for coordinated consultant teams.

Working collaboratively with builders, architects, planners, surveyors, landscape architects, and other project consultants.
