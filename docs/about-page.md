# Why We Exist

Engineering plays an important role in shaping our communities, but for many homeowners and small developers, finding the right engineering support can be surprisingly difficult.

Larger firms are often structured around major developments and long-term assignments. Smaller residential projects still require professional attention, careful design, and a clear understanding of municipal requirements.

Pascoe Engineering was founded to help fill that gap.

Our goal is to provide practical civil engineering services that are responsive, approachable, and tailored to each client’s needs. We believe engineering should help people move their projects forward, not leave them feeling overwhelmed by technical language or complicated approval processes.

Whether you are planning a backyard improvement, building a custom home, or advancing a residential development, we are committed to providing clear advice, thoughtful design, and dependable support throughout the process.

# Meet the Founder

## Matthew Pascoe, P.Eng.

Matthew founded Pascoe Engineering after recognizing that homeowners and small developers often struggled to find responsive engineering support for smaller projects.

Drawing on experience in municipal development engineering and residential development, he established Pascoe Engineering to provide practical engineering advice, thoughtful design, and clear communication throughout the approval process.

His approach is built on collaboration, professionalism, and helping clients understand the engineering decisions that shape their projects.
