# Pascoe Engineering Services

## Mission

The Services section helps visitors quickly identify the engineering support that best matches their project.

Rather than presenting a list of engineering services, this section is organized around the types of projects our clients are planning.

Our goal is to help visitors understand where they fit, what services they may require, and where to learn more.

---

# Service Architecture

The Services section is organized into three practice areas.

## Homeowner Projects

Engineering support for residential property improvements.

Typical projects include:

- Swimming pools
- Home additions
- Drainage improvements
- Driveway modifications
- Retaining walls
- Lot grading
- Site alteration support

---

## Land Development

Civil engineering support for small land development projects.

Typical projects include:

- Residential developments
- Commercial developments
- Mixed-use developments
- Institutional projects
- Industrial developments
- Site redevelopment
- Severances
- Infill developments

Typical services include:

- Functional Servicing Reports
- Stormwater Management Reports
- Servicing Plans
- Erosion & Sediment Control Plans
- Municipal engineering coordination

---

## Engineering Support

Professional engineering guidance throughout planning, design, and municipal approvals.

Typical services include:

- Development application support
- Engineering review
- Municipal coordination
- Consultant coordination
- Technical advice
- Design coordination

# Writing Principles

Write for people planning projects.

Avoid assuming readers understand engineering terminology.

Explain services through practical examples.

Focus on helping clients understand what they need—not simply listing what we provide.
