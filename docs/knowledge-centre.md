# Pascoe Engineering Knowledge Centre

## Mission

The Knowledge Centre exists to provide homeowners, builders, architects, planners, and small developers with clear, practical information about civil engineering.

Rather than simply describing our services, the Knowledge Centre explains engineering concepts, municipal approval processes, and common project requirements in straightforward language that helps clients make informed decisions.

Every article should educate first and sell second.

---

# Goals

• Build trust before a client contacts us.

• Become a reliable resource for residential civil engineering information.

• Demonstrate technical expertise through practical explanations.

• Improve search engine visibility by answering real questions people ask.

• Support our service pages with educational content.

---

# Writing Principles

Every article should be:

✓ Accurate

✓ Practical

✓ Easy to understand

✓ Professionally written

✓ Evergreen

Avoid:

• Unnecessary technical jargon

• Clickbait titles

• Long introductions

• Sales-focused writing

Our goal is to become the most helpful engineering resource for homeowners and small developers.

---

# Article Structure

Every article should generally follow this format:

1. Hero
2. Quick Answer
3. Why It Matters
4. Detailed Explanation
5. Common Situations
6. Frequently Asked Questions
7. Related Articles
8. Related Services
9. Call to Action

# Editorial Rules

Every article should answer a real question.

If someone searches Google, they should find exactly the page they need.

Articles should never exist simply to improve SEO.

Instead, SEO should be the result of creating genuinely useful content.

Every article should end by helping readers determine whether professional engineering services may be appropriate for their project.
