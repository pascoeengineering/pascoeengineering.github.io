Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with Pascoe Engineering.

What areas do you serve?

Pascoe Engineering provides civil engineering consulting services in Ontario.

Do you prepare stamped engineering drawings?

Where required and appropriate, engineering drawings and reports can be prepared under the direction of a licensed professional engineer.

Can you help with municipal comments?

Yes. Pascoe Engineering can assist with reviewing municipal comments, preparing responses, and coordinating revised submissions.

What information is needed to start?

Typical starting materials include a survey, architectural/site plan, municipal comments, servicing information, and any previous reports or drawings.
