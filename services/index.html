Homeowner Projects

Engineering support for residential property improvements.

Residential projects often require careful grading, drainage, and coordination with municipal permit requirements. Pascoe Engineering provides practical plans and clear guidance to help homeowners move their projects forward.

01

Pool Grading Plans

Grading plans for new swimming pools, pool removals, and associated backyard improvements. The plan demonstrates how surface drainage will be managed while protecting neighbouring properties and existing drainage patterns.

Typical Projects

  • New swimming pools
  • Pool removals
  • Backyard landscaping associated with a pool
  • Pool permit applications
  • Drainage improvements around an existing pool
02

Lot Grading Plans

Grading plans that establish proposed elevations, slopes, drainage directions, and relationships between a residential property and the surrounding lands.

Typical Projects

  • New homes
  • Residential additions
  • Infill development
  • Major landscaping projects
  • Permit and municipal submissions
03

Drainage Plans

Site-specific reviews and plans addressing drainage concerns, proposed improvements, and the movement of surface water across a residential property.

Typical Projects

  • Backyard drainage concerns
  • Water flowing toward a building
  • Impacts on neighbouring properties
  • Regrading and landscaping
  • Drainage improvements associated with construction
04

Driveway Grading Plans

Engineering plans supporting new or modified driveways while considering property drainage, boulevard grades, garage elevations, and municipal requirements.

Typical Projects

  • Driveway widening
  • Driveway relocation
  • New driveway entrances
  • Garage additions
  • Changes to existing property access

Residential Development

Civil engineering design for small residential developments.

Pascoe Engineering prepares the drawings, reports, and technical analysis needed to support small residential developments and municipal approval applications.

01

Grading Plans

Detailed grading design establishing proposed elevations, slopes, drainage routes, retaining requirements, and connections to surrounding lands.

Typical Projects

  • Small subdivisions
  • Infill developments
  • Townhouse projects
  • Severed residential lots
  • Redevelopment sites
02

Servicing Plans

Design of water, sanitary, and storm servicing required to support a proposed development and connect it to available municipal infrastructure.

Typical Projects

  • New residential developments
  • Infill and intensification projects
  • Service connections
  • Internal site servicing
  • Municipal servicing extensions
03

Functional Servicing Reports

Engineering reports evaluating how a proposed development will be serviced by municipal water, sanitary, stormwater, and drainage infrastructure.

Typical Projects

  • Official Plan and Zoning By-law Amendments
  • Draft Plans of Subdivision
  • Site Plan Applications
  • Consent and severance applications
  • Development feasibility reviews
04

Stormwater Management Reports

Technical analysis and design addressing stormwater quantity, quality, runoff controls, and safe drainage for a proposed development.

Typical Projects

  • New residential development
  • Redevelopment and intensification
  • Increased impervious area
  • Municipal approval applications
  • Sites requiring on-site stormwater controls
05

Erosion and Sediment Control Plans

Plans identifying temporary measures used to control erosion and prevent sediment from leaving a construction site during site preparation and construction.

Typical Projects

  • Site clearing and grading
  • Residential construction
  • Development servicing
  • Municipal permit requirements
  • Sites near roads, sewers, or natural features
06

Integrated Support

Coordinated civil design from concept through approval.

Grading, servicing, stormwater, and erosion-control requirements are interconnected. Coordinating these elements together helps identify conflicts early and supports a clearer municipal submission.

Engineering Support

Clear guidance through technical and municipal requirements.

Development projects often involve multiple consultants, municipal requirements, technical comments, and changing design information. Pascoe Engineering helps clients understand what is required and coordinate the work needed to move the project forward.

01

Development Application Support

Engineering assistance throughout the planning and development application process, including coordination of required studies, drawings, technical submissions, and responses to review comments.

02

Municipal Coordination

Communication and coordination with municipal staff, reviewing agencies, planners, architects, surveyors, and other project consultants to help keep technical requirements aligned.

03

Engineering Review

Review of existing drawings, reports, servicing concepts, drainage concerns, and technical requirements to identify key issues, submission gaps, and practical next steps.

04

Design Coordination

Coordination between civil engineering design and the work of architects, planners, landscape architects, surveyors, and other professionals involved in the project.
