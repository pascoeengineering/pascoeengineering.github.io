Project Journey

Support through each stage of the process.

Not every project follows the same path, but most move through a similar sequence of planning, design, review, and implementation.

Idea

Understand the project.

Define the property, project goals, constraints, and likely approval requirements.

Planning

Identify what is needed.

Review available information and determine which drawings, reports, surveys, and approvals may be required.

Engineering

Prepare the design.

Develop the required grading, servicing, drainage, calculations, reports, and supporting documentation.

Municipal Review

Respond and coordinate.

Support submissions, address technical comments, and coordinate revisions with the project team.

Implementation

Move the project forward.

Provide clarification and engineering support as the approved design progresses toward construction.
